Fairplay in Pokemon TCG

Cheating

Cheating in Pokemon TCG is a huge issue that isn't properly addressed by the Pokemon Company. Inconsistent and biased judging, combined with slap-on-the-wrist bans, have made cheating abundant at the highest level of the game. People even felt comfortable cheating at the World Championship, only getting a Disqualification and not a ban.
